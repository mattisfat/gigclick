Sample-accurate click
Timing comes from the audio hardware clock, not app timers. No drift, no stutter, 20–300 BPM — and it keeps playing with the screen off.
Readable from the throne
A huge beat counter fills the screen; beat one flashes in your accent colour. Full-screen gig mode strips everything back to the count, your setlist, and a big start/stop.
Built for the gig
A song library with tempo, time signature and subdivision per song. Setlists you can build, reorder, and step through with big previous/next buttons — plus a printable PDF export for the rest of the band.
Practice tools
Subdivisions, per-beat accents and mutes, gap training that drops the click out for a few bars, a tempo trainer that nudges the BPM up, and tap tempo.
No nonsense
No ads, no subscriptions, no account, no data collected — your songs and setlists stay on your device.
